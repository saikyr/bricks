Ball Type Visual Preview

Each ball animates in real-time with trail. Normal at base size; special balls at 1.6x.

Normal Ball

Plain white sphere. Intentionally simple baseline.

Normal

white sphere + cyan glow
  • White spherical gradient
  • Specular highlight dot
  • Cyan glow halo
  • Fading dot trail

Spectral Ball

Ghost/ethereal ball that phases through enemies. Should look translucent and otherworldly.

A: Plasma Ghost

baseline — more glow, smoother blobs
  • Teal shell with bright edge ring
  • 3 plasma blobs orbit inside (smooth)
  • Strong outer glow halo
  • Pulsing plasma-streaked trail

B: Plasma Ghost v2

fewer blobs, bigger + softer, more glow
  • 2 larger, softer plasma blobs
  • Wider diffuse glow (stronger halo)
  • Thicker shell fill, softer edge
  • Smoother blob motion, gentler trail

C: Plasma Ghost v3

fast blobs, electric edge sparks
  • 4 small fast plasma blobs
  • Electric sparks on shell edge
  • Brighter center glow
  • Energetic pulsing trail

D: Plasma Ghost v4

single swirling core, heavy glow
  • One large plasma mass swirling inside
  • Very strong outer glow
  • Shell barely visible — all glow
  • Warm white-teal dreamy trail

Laser Ball

Fires a beam on every hit perpendicular to travel. Purple plasma grenade style.

Laser

purple plasma + energy rings
  • Purple-white body with bright center
  • Concentric energy rings pulse outward
  • Body throbs with energy
  • Hot ripple ring trail

Bleed Ball

Stacks bleed on hit. Stacks pop for bonus damage when enemy hit by any ball.

Bleed

deep red sphere + dripping trail
  • Deep crimson red sphere
  • Pulsing red border
  • Dripping dot trail
  • Red glow halo

Burn Ball

Sets enemies on fire — 3s DoT. Flickering flame design.

Burn

flickering flame + ember trail
  • Flickering orange-yellow body
  • 4 flame wisps orbiting
  • Ember square trail
  • Warm glow halo

Poison Ball

5s poison DoT. Spreads to nearby enemies on kill.

Poison

bubbling green + drip trail
  • Toxic green sphere with surface bubbles
  • Green drip trail
  • Green glow halo
  • Specular highlight

Lightning Ball

Chains to 3 nearby enemies on hit. Electric blue style.

Lightning

arcing electricity + spark trail
  • Bright white-blue body
  • Small arcs off surface
  • Spark trail
  • Blue-white glow halo

Freeze Ball

30% chance to freeze for 2s. Frozen enemies take 1.5x damage.

Freeze

crystalline ice + snowflake trail
  • Icy white-cyan body
  • Crystalline star facets
  • Snowflake/crystal trail
  • Cool blue glow

Explosive Ball

AoE splash on every hit. Should feel hot, dangerous, volatile.

A: Cannonball

hot gradient + corona wisps
  • Yellow-white to dark red gradient
  • 5 flame wisps orbit outside
  • Pulsing radius +/- 6%
  • Ember square trail + double spark

B: Bomb

dark iron shell, glowing fuse spark
  • Dark metallic iron-grey sphere
  • Bright fuse spark on top, spitting sparks
  • Faint orange underglow seeping out
  • Spark shower trail

C: Plasma Grenade

current — pulsing energy charge about to blow
  • Orange-red core with bright white center
  • Concentric energy rings pulse outward
  • Body throbs with energy buildup
  • Hot ripple trail

D: Meteor

burning rock hurtling through space
  • Rocky textured orange-brown sphere
  • Bright leading edge from friction
  • Flame wake trailing behind
  • Smoke + ember particle trail
